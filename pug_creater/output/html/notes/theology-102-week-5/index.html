CHRISTOLOGY PT.2

These are the following learning that I was grateful to learn about this week:

  1. Christ is eternally begotten from the Father, this is not speaking that Christ had a beginning because that goes against John 1:2-3
  2. Begotten in an eternal sense is speaking of the relationship of the First and Second person of the Trinity
  3. The Holy Spirit is not begotten, but rather procession
  4. When talking about order in the Trinity we are not speaking of glory, majesty, or essence, but rather with relationship
  5. The word Trinity is not found in found in Scripture but it is based on the biblical verbiage presented
  6. The doctrine of the Trinity was formally articulated by the Councils of Nicea(AD 325) and Constantinople(AD 381), but weren't invented here
  7. The term arised to combat heresies that were arising from Gnosticism and Monarchianism
  8. Christ (Second person of the Trinity) was sent from the Father as a result of God's love for mankind (John 3:16)
  9. Christ has always existed as the Son of God but became a child only at the moment of His miraculous conception
  10. "Son of God" title was understood categorically by everyone as a title of deity
  11. Theophany meaning appearance of God usually is referred to the old testament and are seen in passage like Gen. 16: 7-3 and has to be seen through the author rather than the character
  12. As we read John 1:18 we see that "no one has seen God...,he has made him known" made known in greek ( ex geomai )
  13. Jesus (the Son of God) made the Father known to mankind
  14. We see Christ intervening in history when mankind rebelled along with establishing the Kingdom of God on earth
  15. The point of the word "God-breathed" points to the origin of Scripture which is the divine breath of the Holy Spirit
  16. The Son of God appears as the one speaking to the people both in the OT and NT
  17. Christ is the theophany giving revelation by means of His personal presence
  18. The Spirit plays a key role in the prophets' recording of the revelation they seen
  19. He who is the Word of God speaks all thing into existence and pronounces judgement

Sola Scriptura ( Scripture Alone ), Solus Christus ( Christ Alone ), Sola fide ( Faith Alone ), Sola Gratia ( Grace Alone ), and Soli Deo Gloria ( Glory to God Alone )
