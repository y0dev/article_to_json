Week 5: Humans and Sin, Angels and Demons

These are the following learning that I was grateful to learn about this week:

  1. Satan can serve God's purposes in the realm of church discipline when repentance remains absent
  2. Believers are to gird up the loins with truth which show they have a heart for battle because of their commitment to Christ
  3. God has provided the breastplate of righteousness to protect the believer's mind and emotions
  4. Though Satan and one-third of the angel disqualified themselves from servest to the LORD, they weren't completely banned from heaven
  5. Satan will only bruise Christ heel (cause Him to suffer), while Christ will bruise Satan's head (destroy him with a fatal blow)
  6. When someone is demonize, the demon exercise living and dominant control over that person
  7. The sealing ministry of the Holy Spirit protects Christians against demon invasion, and its unbiblical and impossible for a true believer to have a demon invasion
  8. Demonization refers to the only unbelievers in whom a demon resides
  9. Believers can be tormented, oppressed, and harassed externally, even to to severe degree like Saul
  10. A demon is far more powerful than a human so we must trust the appeal to God in prayer to deal with situation of demonization
  11. The angel of the LORD has been identified as a special created angel, some say Michael the archangel, but no created angel has ever show traits of deity
  12. Also the angel of the LORD is a self-manifestation of Yahweh himself
  13. The angel of the LORD showed traits of deity (Ex, 3:2-5; Judg. 13:17-18; Ex. 23:21;33:14; Isa. 63:9)
  14. The identification of the angel of the LORD matches the NT explanation of the preincarnate Christ
  15. The words uttered by Christ asserts that He was the angel of the LORD mentioned in the OT because it asserts that more than one person can be God
  16. The attributes of the OT angel of the LORD compare perfectly with those of Christ

I still have questions on the following:

  1. How can demons be pictured in today's age, such as in a homeless person who is talking to themselves, or can it be your average person?
  2. If one is demon possessed what do we do as Christians?
  3. Will the Antichrist be some type of leader like the king of Babylon in Isaiah 14?
  4. What portion in man's sinful action is Satan or demon deception; or just the man's own sinfulness? (The Devil made me do it theology)
