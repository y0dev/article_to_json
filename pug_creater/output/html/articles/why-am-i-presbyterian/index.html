Why am I Presbyterian

There is an endless debate on covenant membership. Who is a part of the covenant and who is not? This question is a good question to cause you to search the scriptures for answers. I would start from Genesis and end in Revelation.

Sola Scriptura ( Scripture Alone ), Solus Christus ( Christ Alone ), Sola fide ( Faith Alone ), Sola Gratia ( Grace Alone ), and Soli Deo Gloria ( Glory to God Alone )
